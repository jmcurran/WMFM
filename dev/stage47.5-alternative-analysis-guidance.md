# Stage 47.5: Alternative-analysis guidance

Stage 47.5 identifies questions that are meaningful but cannot be answered by
an ordinary interpretation of the current fitted model.

The shared route contract now gives deterministic guidance for:

- probability questions about crossing a threshold when the current target is
  a continuous outcome mean;
- conditional-distribution and conditional-quantile questions;
- causal questions that cannot be resolved from an adjusted association alone;
  and
- model-adequacy or diagnostic questions that require residual and influence
  assessment.

These questions use the `alternative_analysis_needed` route. WMFM explains the
analysis or evidence required, but does not silently refit a different model,
manufacture a probability, inspect diagnostics that were not computed, or turn
an observational association into a causal claim.

Existing deterministic prediction, interval, residual-ranking,
comparable-observation, adjustment-comparison, clarification, purpose, and
statistical-education pathways remain unchanged.
