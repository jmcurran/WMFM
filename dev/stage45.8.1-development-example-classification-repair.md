# Stage 45.8.1: Development example classification repair

Stage 45.8.1 repairs the lower- and higher-residual development examples so their follow-up questions use the established Stage 45 classifier vocabulary.

The original fixtures inserted "the model" between "worse than" or "better than" and "expected". Those formulations fell outside the deliberately conservative deterministic classifier and were routed as unsupported.

The repaired examples now use canonical phrases already covered by the observation-residual characterisation tests:

- "Find the students who performed worse than expected."; and
- "Which students performed better than expected?".

No production classifier, residual computation, deterministic answer, or presentation behaviour is changed.
